About Rouma Pty Ltd

Founded in South Africa, Rouma Pty Ltd specializes in financial education, skills development, and applied research. Our mission is to equip individuals and organizations with the tools they need to thrive in a rapidly changing economy.

We focus on:

  • Delivering practical financial training programs
  • Conducting research to support informed decision-making
  • Partnering with businesses to build sustainable strategies
  • Empowering communities through knowledge-sharing

Our team is committed to integrity, innovation, and collaboration — values that guide every project we undertake.
