Who We Are

Rouma Pty Ltd is a Cape Town–based company specializing in financial skills development and research. We partner with individuals, professionals, and organizations to strengthen financial literacy, enhance decision-making, and drive sustainable growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people and businesses with practical financial knowledge and research-driven insights. We believe that strong financial skills are the foundation of long-term success and resilience in today’s economy.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We provide honest, transparent, and ethical guidance.
  • Innovation: We use modern tools and research methods to deliver impactful learning.
  • Collaboration: We partner with clients to co-create solutions tailored to their needs.

Our Expertise

At Rouma Pty Ltd, our team combines expertise in finance, economics, and research. We deliver training programs, workshops, and consulting services that bridge the gap between theory and practice, ensuring our clients gain skills they can apply immediately.
